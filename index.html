Explore how societies transform over time through culture, technology, and human interaction.

What Is Social Change?

Social change refers to significant alterations over time in behavior patterns, cultural values, norms, and social structures within a society.

Factors Causing Social Change

  • Technological innovation
  • Population growth and migration
  • Cultural interaction
  • Economic development
  • Environmental changes

Impact of Social Change

Social change can improve quality of life, create new opportunities, but also bring challenges such as inequality or cultural conflict.
